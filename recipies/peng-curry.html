Peng's Curry!

Description

This Japanese Curry recipie will provide a meal for a whole house!

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Carrots
  • Mushrooms
  • Onions
  • Chicken Broth
  • S&B Golden Curry Sauce Mix
  • Rice

Steps

  1. Cube up all vegetable ingredients
  2. Cook off the raw edge off vegetables
  3. Pour in Broth to help get fonde off surface of pan
  4. Bring broth to a simmer and cover with lid for 10 minutes
  5. In a seperate pot, add water or chicken broth to break down the Golden Curry Sauce Blocks
  6. After the 10 minutes and blocks are dissolved, combine in the bigger pot
  7. Allow to thicken to desired viscosity and serve with rice!
